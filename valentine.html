sabse khoobsurat surprise ho,
meri hasi ki wajah,
meri duniya ka sabse pyara hissa!

jaise chaand bina chandni ke,
ya gulab bina khushbu ke! 🌹

mera sabse pyara sapna jo poora ho gaya!
I love you, my vanshu! 💕
