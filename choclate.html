Happy Chocolate, My Chocolate! 🌹

"my kitkat! 🍫🔥❤
You know, chocolates come in different flavors—sweet, dark,
nutty… but my favorite is you—the perfect mix of sweet and a little spicy! 😘
If I were there, I wouldn’t just give you chocolates…
I’d slowly feed you one,
watching you take each bite while
I steal a few of my own—maybe from your lips too. 😏🔥
But since we’re apart, just know that my love for you
melts faster than chocolate in my hands!
🍫💖 So, keep my share safe… or be ready to make it up to
me in the most delicious way possible when we meet! 😉😘" 💕🔥 💖

Aap meri life ki sabse meethi mithaas ho,
jaise chocolate har pal ko aur khaas
banata hai… waise hi aap meri har yaadon ko special banati ho.
Apki muskurahat ek melted chocolate ki tarah hoti hai
—soft, smooth, aur irresistible.🍫❤
